Geoglyths in Peru

This map is inspired by a family trip traveling to Peru a few years ago. We had the chance to see the geoglyths along The Nazca Lines.

I am wishing to expand the map more extensively on introducing more of the geoglyths around the world.

About the geoglyths along The Nazca Lines:

  • Massive geoglyphs in Nazca desert.
  • Created by Nazca culture, 500 BCE - 500 CE.
  • Depict animals, plants, geometric shapes.
  • Only visible from above.
  • Purpose remains a mystery.
